ELDOME

Elderly Care: 2023.07 - 2023.10

Project Overview

ELDOME is an innovative mobile application designed to enhance social activities for the elderly in New Zealand, addressing new methods for collecting well-being metrics. It non-invasively gathers data and strengthens connections between the elderly and younger generations, improving their quality of life and emotional health.

Personal Contributions:

Detailed Requirement Analysis

Systematically analyzed the needs of elderly users, precisely defining feature requirements through multiple rounds of prototyping and user feedback loops.

Agile Development Practice

Adopted agile development methods to rapidly iterate on product features, effectively shortening the development cycle and increasing flexibility in responding to changes.

User Experience Optimization

Continuously optimized the user interface and interaction processes based on user behavior data and feedback, ensuring the app is intuitive and meets the special needs of elderly users.

Usability Testing

Designed and conducted a series of usability tests to validate the effectiveness of the interface design, ensuring it is easy for elderly users to master.
